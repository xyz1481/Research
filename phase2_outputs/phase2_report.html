Sub-task 1 — Disruption Label Definition

Delay Distribution Histogram

Threshold Justification

Formula: delay = Actual_Lead_Time − Scheduled_Lead_Time

Threshold: > 2 days → disruption = 1

Rationale: Industry standard SLA buffers tolerate up to 2 days variance without triggering penalty clauses (Dolgui et al., 2020, Int. J. Production Research). The histogram confirms a natural density gap at this boundary.

Result:
  Class 0 (no disruption): 8,982 (89.8%)
  Class 1 (disruption): 1,018 (10.2%)
